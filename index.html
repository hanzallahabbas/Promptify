How it works
01 — DETECT
Pattern matching
Regex engine scans your prompt for emails, phones, CNICs, and card numbers.
02 — CLASSIFY
Intent analysis
Checks if you're sharing your own data or just asking about public information.
03 — SCORE
Risk scoring
Assigns a 0–100 risk score based on the type and count of sensitive items found.
04 — SANITIZE
Safe masking
Replaces sensitive data with labels like [EMAIL] while keeping your prompt's meaning.
